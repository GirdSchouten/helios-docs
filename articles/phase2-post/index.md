## Building on Our Foundation

Our goal is to create a blockchain that combine automation, AI, and interoperability — a chain designed for the next generation of decentralized applications.  

That vision remains at the core of everything we do. What’s new today is the **next step forward**: Helios as the **ETF-native blockchain**.  

By making ETFs, stables, and RWAs on-chain primitives, Helios extends its foundation of interchain automation and reputation-based consensus into a model that unifies liquidity and brings real-world scale to Web3.  

---

## From Fragmentation to Portfolios

Crypto today is fragmented. Liquidity is scattered across tokens, chains, and apps. Retail users chase single assets, while institutions hesitate because there’s no simple way to own the market.  

Helios fixes this by making **portfolios the base layer**:  

- Stake ETH, BTC, BNB, AVAX, MATIC, stables, or RWAs.  
- Receive liquid ETF tokens that represent diversified baskets.  
- Let Chronos AI rebalance allocations and distribute profits like dividends.  
- Bridge portfolios across ecosystems with Hyperion for trading, collateral, and DeFi use.  

Just like ETFs transformed TradFi by making investing simple, **Helios ETFs aim to onboard the next wave of users into DeFi**.
